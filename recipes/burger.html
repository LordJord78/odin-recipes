Burger

Keys to Success

Butter that pan up real good!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayo

Instructions for cooking

  1. Form Patties: Divide beef into 4 equal parts and shape into patties. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook: Grill or pan-fry over medium-high heat for 3–4 mins per side (add cheese during the last minute if using).
  3. Toast Buns: Lightly toast buns for 1–2 minutes.
  4. Assemble: Add patty to bun, top with lettuce, tomato, onion, and your favorite condiments.
  5. Serve: Enjoy hot with fries or a cold drink!
